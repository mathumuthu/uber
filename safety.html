RIDE SAFE

Safety Tips for Riders

Verify Captain

Always check the captain's photo, name, and vehicle details match the app before starting the ride.

Wear Helmet

Always wear the helmet provided by the captain. It's mandatory and for your protection.

Share Trip

Share your live trip status with family or friends, especially for night rides or new routes.

Stay Alert

Keep your phone charged and accessible. Use the SOS button if you feel unsafe at any point.
